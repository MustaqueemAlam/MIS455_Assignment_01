Welcome to Manchester United

Latest updates, news, and events about Manchester United.

Latest news updates about the club. As of June 2024, Manchester United has been active in both transfer markets and on the pitch, with significant developments affecting the club's immediate future.

Departures and Arrivals: Several players have left United, including Phil Jones, whose contract ended, and loanees like Marcel Sabitzer and Wout Weghorst returning to their parent clubs. On the incoming side, United sees the return of Alex Telles and Eric Bailly from loan spells, which could impact the squad depth (Sky Sports).

Transfers and Rumors: Manchester United is reportedly exploring a high-profile move for Neymar, which could be one of the blockbuster deals of the summer. However, the club is considering a loan arrangement rather than a permanent transfer to mitigate financial risks. Alongside this, United has shown interest in Chelsea midfielder Mason Mount. Mount's potential transfer is significant as he could strengthen United's midfield, which already includes key players like Casemiro and Christian Eriksen (Independent, Sky Sports).

Achievements and Competitions: Manchester United qualified for the 2024/25 Europa League by winning the FA Cup, defeating Manchester City 2-1 in a thrilling final. Goals from Alejandro Garnacho and Kobbie Mainoo secured the victory, ensuring United finished the season with silverware. This win is particularly sweet as it comes against their local rivals, cementing a successful campaign under Erik ten Hag's management (Notjustok).
